Tale Factory

“There is no greater agony than bearing an untold story inside you.”

Maya Angelou

Everything in life is writable about, if you have the outgoing guts to do it, and the imagination to improvise. The worst enemy to creativity is self-doubt. Tale Factory is a short story writing competition where you can put self-doubt to bed and let rivers of creativity flow.

RULES
  • The word limit is 500-700 words. There would be a penalty for exceeding the word limit.
  • Artistic illustrations are also allowed.
  • Use of vulgar language will lead to disqualification.
  • Points will be awarded on the basis of innovation, depiction of the situation, creativity and use of good language.
  • The medium of communication must be English.
  • Deadline for the submissions is 11:59 PM on 28th March 2021.
  • Results will be announced on 4th April 2021 through our Instagram handle
  • Prizes:
    • Winner: INR 7,000
    • Runner-Up: INR 3,000
