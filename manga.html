Manga India

Get ready to connect your heart and soul with Manga, the very own indigenous “rock n roll” band. Don’t miss this musical extravaganza from the Indian heartland and its innumerably diverse facets. In a sea of indistinguishable music, their compositions have a sense of nostalgia from the 80's-90's era . The band's sound is sculpted from their differences, with each member bringing out their individual tastes and sonic persona, resulting in a multi-genre anthology of songs .Get ready to go berserk with their amazing amalgamations to have you vibe along.
