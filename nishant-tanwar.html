Nishant Tanwar

Adding to the grandeur, we bring to you the much-awaited event. We are here with one of the most celebrated comedians, the one and only, Nishant Tanwar. He is a quintessential Delhi guy and this 'Jokesingh' serves his take on comedy, with a hard-to-resist style of humour that will have you nodding your heads and tickling your funny bones as you will relate to everything he says with his quickest wits and dankest content on stand-up comedy. Gear up for this comic ride and cherish the moments of laughter and joy as we refuse to spare you even a moment of boredom.

Details
  • Date: 28th March 2021, Sunday
  • Venue: Football Ground, IIITB
  • Time: 6:00 PM

IIIT Bangalore is back with a bash with its 6th iteration of INFIN8, better than ever before. Hold on to your seats as we present to you the biggest and the most exciting fest of our institute.

Come!! Be a part of this fun ride along with us @IIIT Bangalore.
