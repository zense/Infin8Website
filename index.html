F.A.Q

  • Where can we register for the events?

    Head to the official website of Infin8 and go to respective event you want to participate in, then register by filling up the form given at the site.

  • Can we participate in both online and offline events?

    Online events: anyone can participate on the basis of the category - Open to all events/only for IIITB students.
    Offline events: explicitly for students present on campus.

  • How will I receive my certificates and prizes?

    For prizes, winners will be asked their account details and they'll receive exciting cash prizes directly to their accounts.

  • Deadline to register?

    Each event has its own deadline for registering/submitting. Make sure you keep yourself updated with the dates of events and their last dates of submission.
    Go to the respective event section and check the rules for further details.

  • How to attend/spectate all the events?

    The event link will be sent to the registered participants well in advance. Further rules for the winners as well as the students of IIITB will be informed.

  • Will we get participation certificates?

    Digital participation certificates will be given to all participants.

  • What timings are the events held at?

    Timings of all the events will be given on the official website of Infin8. Go to schedule section on the website to find the event you are interested in.

  • Will I be able to come and physically attend all the events?

    We at IIIT Bangalore are trying are level best to follow all the norms specified by the government and since we have too many constraints, we are taking all the possible precautions.
    So, unfortunately you cannot visit the campus physically.

  • By when will the results be announced of each competition?

    Results announcement dates are mentioned in the rules section of individual events. Kindly check on the official website of Infin8!
