EVENT DETAILS

Please read the following instructions carefully before registering for the event.




Valorant

Gaming brings out emotions uniquely and beautifully. Experience an intense gaming atmosphere with everything from clutch victories to rage quits. Whether you are a veteran with numerous hours under your belt or a newcomer, the joy in gaming with mates is the same!. Remember talent wins games but teamwork wins tournaments. Gather your comrades and give all you've got!

RULES
  • Team Size should be of 5 members. Not Less, Not More.
  • Team Name is Mandatory.
  • ID proof(Aadhar Card) required for students not from IIITB.
  • Deadline for registration is 21st March 2021.
  • All participants are supposed to have an account on discord.
  • Participants should have their real names on Discord Server. No nicknames are allowed.
  • Deadline to join the Discord Server is 22nd March 2021. Server link will be sent to the participants.
  • All communication regarding the tournament will be done on discord server only.
  • Team will be banned from the tournament if the organising committee found any malpractice
  • No use of abusive language.
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the game before the day of the match. No delays will be allowed on the day of the match.
  • All games will be knockout matches.
  • Preliminary matches would be held on 24th and 25th of March while Semifinals and Finals will be held on 26th and 27th of March respectively
  • Prizes:
    • Winner: INR 25,000*

    • * Final amount to be decided on the basis of participation.
