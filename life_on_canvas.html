Please read the following instructions carefully before registering for the event.

Entrants confirm that everyone whose submission is featured in the contest entry has given consent for the use of his/her submission in connection with this Contest and in accordance with these Terms and Conditions.

  • All artists will retain full ownership of their submissions but, by entering, you agree to allow us to use the entries in the promotion of the Infin8. Any personal information collected from the participants will be used only for the purposes of administering the contest.
  • The decision of the organising committee and the jury will be final.

Life on a Canvas

Every painted canvas is a window to a timeless reality where the soul of the artist resonates with the soul of an admirer. It is difficult to portray “Life on a Canvas”, but here we are! .Step into your imagination and splash your thoughts and emotions onto a canvas. The best painting based on parameters such as creativity, meaning conveyed, execution will win.

RULES
  • This is a solo participation event.
  • Submitted photographs should be of good quality.
  • Appropriate level of uniform illumination with white light (sunlight).
  • Parallax should be minimal, digital touch-ups beyond cropping and rotation are not allowed.
  • Dimensions of the original art should be mentioned.
  • The sources of elements of the artwork if copied or inspired from external sources should be cited.
  • Basic editing, including colour enhancement, the use of filters, and cropping of the submission(s) is acceptable, provided any such editing does not affect the authenticity and/or genuineness of the submission(s).
  • Advanced editing used to create illusions, deceptions and/or manipulations, and the adding and removing of significant elements within the frame is prohibited.
  • Participants have to submit a Time-Lapse video along with their artwork.
  • Deadline for first round submissions is 11:59 PM of 25th March 2021.
  • Results will be announced on 27th March 2021.
  • Prizes:
    • Winner: INR 7,000*
    • Runner-Up: INR 3,000*
