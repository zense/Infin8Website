Please read the following instructions carefully before registering for the event.

Entrants confirm that everyone whose submission is featured in the contest entry has given consent for the use of his/her submission in connection with this Contest and in accordance with these Terms and Conditions.

  • All artists will retain full ownership of their submissions but, by entering, you agree to allow us to use the entries in the promotion of the Infin8. Any personal information collected from the participants will be used only for the purposes of administering the contest.
  • The decision of the organising committee and the jury will be final.

Groove n' Glam

Through dance, the mind and soul create an interface with the body to let out the art and culture trapped within. Groove n' Glam - one of our most energetic events is here. A dazzling group dance competition bound to capture hearts and break all linguistic barriers through dance.

RULES
  • Team could have anywhere between 4-10 members.
  • The performance should not exceed 5 minutes.
  • Any dance form is accepted.
  • One entry per team.
  • The submission should be a video format file(preferably .mp4 format).
  • Vulgarity of any kind with respect to dance moves/costumes will lead to disqualification.
  • Basic editing, including colour enhancement, the use of filters, and cropping of the submission is acceptable, provided any such editing does not affect the authenticity and/or genuineness of the submission.
  • Advanced editing used to create illusions, deceptions and/or manipulations, and the adding and removing of significant elements is prohibited.
  • The video should be submitted on the IIIT Bangalore Infin8 website by filling the form.
  • Deadline for the submissions is 11:59 PM on 28th March 2021.
  • Results will be announced on 4th April 2021 through our Instagram handle
  • Prizes:
    • Winner: INR 15,000
    • Runner-Up: INR 10,000
